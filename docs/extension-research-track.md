# Extension & Research Track

The stages in this track go **beyond the K–12 classroom core** (Stages I–IV) into
university-level plant science: hormone signalling, cell culture and cloning, and
computational modelling of roots, whole plants, and metabolism.

!!! warning "Advanced — research tools, some may be offline"
    These stages were written for advanced and research audiences and rely on
    specialist software, some of it hosted by third-party labs. **A few of these
    external tools and demo apps are no longer maintained and may be offline.** The
    science and methods still stand — treat these pages as a reference and a jumping-off
    point rather than a turnkey classroom lesson.

    Teaching K–12? Start with the [Classroom Track (Stages I–IV)](stage-i-scientific-photography/README.md)
    and the [For Teachers](for-teachers.md) guide instead.
