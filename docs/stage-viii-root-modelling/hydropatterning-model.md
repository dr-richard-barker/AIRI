# Hydropatterning model

## Hydropatterning model

Ever wonder how growth and the environment locally affect the water potential of tissues and the patterning of lateral roots? Neil Robbins did and wrote an interactive Shiny app _(no longer online)_ that let you explore a computational model of hydropatterning with the ease of a user friendly GUI. Work described in a recent manuscript submitted to BioRxiv (Robbins II and Dinneny, 2017). Enjoy!
